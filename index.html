About

I'm a 2D & 3D Motion Designer based in Warsaw with 10 years of experience creating dynamic videos for television, social media, advertising, and marketing campaigns.


Currently working in Polish television, I craft engaging animated promotions and high-quality content. I also explore the integration of AI tools into motion design workflows, using emerging technologies to optimize creativity and efficiency.


Whether for broadcast, digital platforms, or brand campaigns — I combine creativity, technical expertise, and AI-driven innovation to bring ideas to life.
